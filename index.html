My Game Development Dream: From Player to Creator

Welcome to my Coursera final project website.

This is not only the test for week 1 but also a place where I share my dream of becoming an indie game developer.

Motivation

In the past, I really enjoyed playing games. Whenever I immersed myself in the virtual world, I felt the joy and excitement that those games brought.

It was those emotions that motivated me: I also wanted to create similar joy for others. I believe that creating a game can provide entertaining experiences and valuable connections.

Dreams and Study Plans

  1. Main goal: After this HTML course, I will take a Unity course on Coursera.
  2. Purpose: To gain experience in order to build my own game project.
  3. Why I chose Coursera: I have taken several Coursera courses before and found them very helpful. I believe that the game development course will greatly assist me in creating games and nurturing my passion for IT.
  4. Game idea: Although I do not have a specific idea for my first game yet, I believe that the experience I gain will help me come up with a unique concept.

Curious about the Gaming Industry.

My interest goes beyond just game programming. I also want to learn more about how major game companies operate.

I want to know about other jobs such as sound design, event organization in games, and creating visual effects. This is a vast field that I want to explore comprehensively.
